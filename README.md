# SnapEats 📸🍎

**SnapEats** is a sleek, AI-powered food tracking and nutrition companion. Snap a photo of your meal, and let SnapEats handle the rest.

## 🚀 Features

- **Food Recognition**: Uses advanced computer vision to identify foods from your camera.
- **Nutritional Insights**: Get instant calorie and macronutrient information.
- **Personalized Profile**: Set your weight, height, and age to get a tailored daily calorie budget.
- **Smart Recommendations**: Suggests recipes and meals based on your history and nutritional needs.
- **Meal History**: Keep track of your past "snaps" and monitor your progress over time.
- **BMI Tracking**: Integrated BMI calculator and history log.

## 📱 Tech Stack

- **UI**: 100% Jetpack Compose for a modern, fluid experience.
- **Database**: Room for local data persistence.
- **Camera**: CameraX for robust and high-performance camera integration.
- **Architecture**: Structured following Clean Architecture and MVVM patterns.

## 🛠️ Getting Started

1. Clone this repository.
2. Open the project in Android Studio.
3. Build and run on a physical device (recommended for camera features).
